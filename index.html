Erik Holverson lives and works in Oakland, California. The multidisciplinary artist began his visual arts journey as a photographer in the 2000s. In addition to painting, he is a songwriter, aspiring DJ, and enjoys creating applications from his terminal.

Holverson paints from the mind and from life around him, occasionally using found imagery for reference. He considers his work abstract figuration and is self-taught. A selection of his completed paintings can be found under Works.
